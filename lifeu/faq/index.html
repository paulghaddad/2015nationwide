FAQ

What is Life University?

Life University is a three-day educational conference for top Nationwide Financial Network (NFN) financial services agents. In 2014, for the eighth year in a row, Life University will mix education and inspiration with recognition for agents from across the country.

When and where is Life University held?

In 2014, Life University will be held August 18 – 20 at the Hyatt Regency, near Nationwide’s headquarters in downtown Columbus, Ohio.

Who can qualify?

It is open to all Exclusive Agency, Allied and Harleysville Insurance agents, registered and non-registered, as well as sales management. Agents who earn additional tickets can bring other licensed producers from their offices. (See qualification criteria for details.)

How do I qualify?

Please see the Requirements page.

What kind of educational sessions are offered?

Life University is your chance to learn from the best of the best. General business sessions feature nationally recognized speakers such as economists, financial industry leaders and motivational speakers. Company leaders also offer insights about Nationwide and the state of the business.

Tactical break-out sessions cover a range of topics, such as products, sales ideas, and proven sales techniques and ideas from other producers — information you’ll be able to use as soon as you return to your office.

Attendees also learn when they network with peers during meals and evening events. In addition, home office contacts such as underwriters and Regional Internal Consultants are on hand so you can meet the person behind the voice on the phone.

Is there any recognition?

Top agents are honored during recognition event. Nationwide leaders and other qualifiers recognize these Dean’s List agency teams as they are called to the stage. All qualifying attendees receive Life University awards to display in their offices.

What other events are included?

On Monday night, a welcoming reception lets qualifiers reconnect with peers and meet agents from across the country. On Tuesday, after a full day of business sessions and breakouts, attendees can relax and unwind at an evening event before the final day of classes.

What does Nationwide pay for?

The company covers one round-trip coach airfare or mileage reimbursement for those who drive to Columbus, hotel room (two nights), meals and evening events.

What if I can't attend Life University?

Another life-licensed agent from your agency can attend in your place.

Will I be compensated if I qualify but I can't attend?

No.
