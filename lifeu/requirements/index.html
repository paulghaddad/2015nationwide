Requirements

To view qualification criteria for 2014 Life University:

Nationwide agents should go to Agent Gateway. On the "Sales" tab, click on "Financial." In the "Sales Support" section, click on "Life University/Leaders Conference" to see "2014 Life University Qualifications."

Allied agents should go to Agent Center. Under "Services," select "Nationwide Life and Annuity" from the drop-down menu. Click on "Nationwide Life Conference Information" to see "2014 Life University Qualifications."

Harleysville agents should to go to accessHarleysville. Under "Life" select Product Sales/Marketing. Click on "Agency Rewards for Life" to see "2014 Life University Qualifications".
