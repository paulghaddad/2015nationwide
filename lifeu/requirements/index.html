2014 Life University Qualifications: Exclusive Agency

Agent

Sales Period: December 16, 2013 – May 31, 2014, or YTD commission production reported as of May 31, 2014

  • Option 1: $24,000 in eligible MDRT qualifiable commissions, with a minimum of $8,800 in life commissions, qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University* OR
  • Option 2: $11,000 in life commissions qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University* OR
  • Option 3: 44 life applications paid and delivered** qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University OR
  • Option 4: $250,000 first-year life premium, excluding internal replacement premium paid**, qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University.

Note: For 2 attendees to qualify, agency must attain 2 times Option 1, Option 2, Option 3 or Option 4 above. For 3 attendees to qualify, agency must attain 3 times Option 1, Option 2, Option 3 or Option 4 above, and so forth.

* Excludes turnkey asset management program (TAMP) and non-variable products distributed through Nationwide-approved BGAs (e.g., Crump, Triple I)

** Life applications paid and delivered are based on life sales as reported through Revenue Connection for the period January 2014 through May 2014

Sales Manager

  • 100% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life commission objective met OR
  • Has 5 primary agents from his/her district qualify

Sales AVP

  • 100% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life commission objective met

Conference Dates

2014 Life University Qualifications: Independent Agency — Allied Agents

Agent

Sales Period: December 28, 2013 – May 31, 2014, or YTD commission production reported as of May 31, 2014

  • Option 1: $10,000 in fixed life commissions qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University* OR
  • Option 2: 40 fixed life applications paid and delivered** qualifies for 1 attendee at Life University

Note: For 2 attendees to qualify, agency must attain 2 times Option 1 or Option 2. For 3 attendees to qualify, agency must attain 3 times Option 1 or Option 2.

* Excludes business not written directly through Nationwide.

** Life applications paid and delivered are based on life sales for the period January 2014 through May 2014.

Sales Manager

  • 100% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met and has at least one agent from his/her territory qualify OR
  • 125% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met.

Sales Director

  • 100% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met and has at least two agents from agencies from his/her territory qualify OR
  • 125% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met.

Sales AVP

  • 100% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met and has at least three agents from an agency from his/her territory qualify OR
  • 125% of year-to-date seasonally adjusted life premium and life sales objectives met.

Conference Dates

August 18 – 20, 2014, Hyatt Regency, Columbus, Ohio

Products underwritten by Nationwide Mutual Insurance Company and Affiliated Companies. Life insurance issued by Nationwide Life Insurance Company, Home Office: Columbus, OH 43215-2220.
